About us



"When musicians and software engineers collaborate, there are no obstacles that can't be overcome."


When we were told in the 'User Interfaces' course that we would have to make our own project, the idea of Skiffle was the first thing that came to our minds. One of the hobbies we share in this group is music, which we enjoy as much composing, playing, or listening to.

Vinyl records have always been a topic of global interest, especially in the music community. Seeing that there was no website that covered the topic with a beautiful interface, we decided to create it ourselves. We love music just like any user who uses this page, which makes this project very personal and we have done it from the heart.

This prototype doesn't need a backend, it's made purely with HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
